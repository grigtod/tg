The Royal Mine was at the heart of the 18th-century industrial revolution in Upper Silesia. Explore a labyrinth of tunnels and discover the monumental steam engines that powered the extraction of lead and silver, a testament to pioneering hydraulic engineering.

## What to Notice

- The underground water management system that made deeper mining possible.
- The industrial architecture connected to lead, silver, and zinc extraction.
- The broader UNESCO-listed landscape linked to the mine and its surface infrastructure.
